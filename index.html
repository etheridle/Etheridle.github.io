Frequently asked questions.
T2X is a highly lucritive Staking DApp platform. Developed from the ideas behind previous staking platforms, T2X was designed with one thing in mind, the STAKERS. By owning T2X tokens and Staking them in our Staking Portal users will receive daily interest on their T2X tokens as well as a percentage of the overall amount of TRX that enters the Auction Lobby daily. Fully transparent and secure everything is controlled by smart contracts on the TRON blockchain. There is no MAX supply and token price is soley derived from buying and selling markets.
T2X tokens are available for Auction in our Staking Portal Auction Lobby or on exchanges when listings available.
By Staking your T2X tokens you start recieving daily interest. In addition to daily interest, Stakers are also rewarded with 95% of all TRX that enters the daily Auction Lobby. T2X will become tradeable on multiple exchanges as continue development of additional smart contract games designed to fuel the T2X Stakers.
Staking is ONLY available in our Staking Portal dapp.

Can I have Multiple Stakes? YES
Can I end my Stake early? YES
Am I penalized for ending Stakes early? YES, 50% of your earned Stakes will be distributed to remaining Stakers. Can I modify my Stake once started? NO
What is Staking Minimum and Maximum duration? 1 Day - 5,555 Days
Maximum number of tokens I can Stake? No Maximum
What do I do when my Stake duration ends? Complete steps by executing the End Stake function. After a 2 week period of not claiming your Ended Stakes you will be charged 1% each week that passes.
Initially Staking will be the main focus of the platform. Shortly after we will begin development of smart contract DApps and Games utlizing T2X tokens.
