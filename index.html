2. What is it «T3D Token»?

T3D is a decentralized infrastructure project created by a professional community in cryptography science, finance and investment-banking.

T3D creates an ecosystem of active crypto-investors based on the TRON blockchain, which can guarantee transparency, safety and liquidity.

T3D owners can get a return in T3D tokens, convertible to TRX when there are new platform users, new services implementation and partnership program’s cooperation.

3. Mathematical model

Commission fee for deposit: 10% of the deposit amount.

Fee for withdrawal: 4% from the amount.

Commission fee for transfer to another wallet: 1% from transferred amount.

Example - You have bought 10 000 T3D for 100 TRX, than 10 TRX will be proportionally distributed through all T3D owners.

33% of 10% charging fees sharing between users who has a referral link.

According to this model token pricing balancing by supply and demand from ecosystem’s users, herewith token is secured from manipulation.
