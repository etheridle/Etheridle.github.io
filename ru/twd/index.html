How it works?

There are 106 countries, all starting from 50 TRX. If you buy a country, to take over the country's ownership, the next player will need to pay 1.6x your price. Once a country ownership changes hand, you will receive 110% of your purchase price (one-time). 5% of the purchase price will go to P3T token owners as dividends. 4% goes to dev fee. 20% of the purchase price will go to the Last-Buyer Pot. If there is no purchase of any country in the next 24 hours, the last buyer take the 60% of Last-Buyer Pot home and 40% of the Pot will go to next round. Once there is a country purchase, the Last-Buyer Pot countdown resets to 24 hours.

The world and continents currently earn a 1% dividend for any country trade taking place below them.
Example:
China sells for 10000 TRX
Owner of North America receives 100 TRX
Owner of The World receives 100 TRX
The world and continents can always be purchased by another person for the next price.

FAQ

5% - Dev comission, 10% - P3T Token owners, 25% - Jackpot

Actually, nothing! Smart contract will be safe with all the balance. And it will continue to pay you every day. It works independent from anyone or anything.
